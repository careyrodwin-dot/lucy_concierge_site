About Your Cabin
🚗 Parking & Driveway
The Cabin is located in the Sylvan Lake Falls subdivision and is nestled in a private setting surrounded by woods. The driveway is short, but steep.

If you have a sports car or don't want to drive down, feel free to park at the top of the driveway. If there is a chance of snow or ice, please park at the top of the driveway.
🗑️ Trash & Recycling
⚠️ Please don't leave any trash bags outside - North Georgia has black bears and they will find the trash!

Upon departure, take all trash to the local recycling center:
• Just North of the Cabin off 441 behind the Post Office
• Go to the bottom of the mountain, left about 1 mile, right at the light
• Hours: Mon–Sat 7AM-7PM, Sun 12PM-5PM

Rabun County does offer recycling - please separate glass, paper and plastic.
🔥 Wood Burning Stove
Feel free to use the firewood provided on the basement level.

Instructions:
• Open the flue by pulling the handle out on the right side of the stove
• Prime the flue by lighting a few pieces of paper at the back of the stove
• If you don't prime the flue, smoke may escape into the Cabin
🚽 Septic System
All plumbing is on septic. Please don't place anything in the toilets except for toilet paper. Anything else may cause a backup.
🐻 Wildlife Note
North Georgia has its share of black bears. Please keep all food and trash secured inside. Never leave trash bags outside.
💡 Porch Lights
Use the left switch on the door to the deck in the downstairs bedroom.
🐦 Hummingbird Feeder
Hummingbird season in North Georgia runs late March to October, with a second season November to mid-March.

The hummingbirds are plentiful and magical in the Summer! Be sure there is sugar water in the feeder to attract them.

Sugar water recipe: ¼ cup sugar + 1 cup boiling water. Cool it down before pouring into the feeder.
🧯 Fire Safety
Fire Blankets (for small fires):
• Kitchen - Lower cabinet under microwave
• Living Room - Top right drawer of TV cabinet

Fire Extinguishers:
• Front door
• Next to kitchen sink

Escape Ladders/Ropes:
In the unlikely event that you would be unable to leave through the front door, escape ladders/ropes are placed under the beds.
🚭 No Smoking

⚠️ Violations will incur a $500 penalty.
