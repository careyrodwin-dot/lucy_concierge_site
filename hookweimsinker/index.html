    🏖️ Beach Rules & Safety Guide
    🚩 Beach Flag System
    Green: Low hazard, calm conditions
    Yellow: Medium hazard, moderate surf/currents
    Red: High hazard, rough conditions
    Double Red: Water CLOSED
    Purple: Dangerous marine life present
    Check Current Flag Status →
    🌊 Rip Current Safety
    If caught in a rip current: Stay calm, don't fight it. Swim parallel to shore until free, then swim back. If you can't escape, float and wave for help.
    📋 Beach Rules
    🚫 No glass containers on beach
    🐕 Dog permits required (Walton County residents only)
    🌿 Stay off the dunes – they protect against storms
    ⛺ Tents must be 10'x10' or smaller, top 1/3 of beach only
    🔊 No amplified sound audible beyond 100 feet
    🧹 Leave no trace – items left overnight will be removed
    ☀️ Sun Protection
    Apply SPF 30+ sunscreen, reapply every 2 hours. Seek shade 10am-4pm. Wear hats & sunglasses.
