    🍷 Utah Liquor Law
    Spirits and wine over 5% ABV can ONLY be purchased at Utah State Liquor Stores — not at grocery stores. Closest: Kimball Junction (M-Sat 11am-10pm, closed Sunday) or Main Street downtown.
